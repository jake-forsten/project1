    • About Me

    My name is Jake Forsten, and I am a guitar player based out of the Knoxville, TN area. I've been working full time as a guitar player gigging, recording, and teaching since 2016. I honed my craft touring in bands in the 2010s, as well as studying under some great teachers out of Atlanta, GA and Birmingham, AL. I have worked with a wide range of fanstasic bands and artists over the years, from pop to indie and rock to country. My goal working with others is always to put the songs first and try and find what works best in the moment, whether it be a record or a live performance. I always love working with new people and making their songs the best they can be.

    • Gear I Use & Endorse

    D'addario

    I am endorsed by D'addario, and use their strings, cables and picks. I also have a lot of their accesories which come very much in handy.

    D'addario
    Duesenberg Starplayer

    Duesenberg builds great instruments, and their Starplayer TV has been my main guitar for some time now. I love how versatile the instruments are and the proprietary nature of all their components and hardware.

    Duesenberg Guitars
    Novo Voltur

    Novo guitars are a more recent discovery for me, but I have been absolutely loving mine since I got it. The build quality is second to none, and I absolutely love the vibe and look of it.

    Novo Voltur
    K Line Tele

    This great tele, built by Chris Kroenline, has been a mainstay for me for the last four years or so. It plays and sounds amazing, and I love how worn in and aged the finish has become.

    K-Line Guitars
    Ebo Amps

    Eric Borasch is an amazing amp tech and builder, who I discovered through his work with the great session player Tom Buvakovac. I use his "Rock Show" head, which is a fantasic Marshall-like amplifier. I can't recommend his work enough!

    Ebo Customs
    Analogman Pedals

    The Analogman King of Tone is probably the greatest pedal of all time. They also build lots of other cool stuff, as well as do some great mods to existing pedals. I recommend every single product they make.

    Analogman

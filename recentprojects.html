    Clive Studios

    Clive Studios is a recent development in the Knoxville music scene, headed by my friend Blake Wrinn, with the primary goal being to bring Nashville's level of recording excellence to the Knoxville scene. The physcial space is still under construction with exciting things to come in 2025, and the collective of studio musicians and talent being brought together there making records is a real honor to be a part of.
